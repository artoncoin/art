    What is the total token supply for this project, and what's the plan for it over 15 years?
    The total token supply is 555 trillion. Over 15 years, 50% of the supply will go into the liquidity pool each year, and 5% of the supply will be burned annually. The details for the rest of the supply are outlined in the project's whitepaper.
    Why should I invest in this project?
    This project offers a unique combination of cryptocurrency art coins and actual artwork on these coins. Holders can trade, buy, and sell these artworks, and they can also hold the tokens in their wallets. The artworks can be sold to art collectors, galleries, and on various art platforms, including NFTs.
    Can holding $ART tokens make me a millionaire, and why?
    The belief is that holding $ART tokens for a minimum of 10 years could potentially make you a millionaire. This is because the project aims for global acceptance within the art community. If artists, art galleries, art communities, and museums worldwide adopt $ART as their art currency, it could lead to widespread use
    How does the project support artists, galleries, and museums?
    The project's founder has reserved a yearly amount to support art galleries and museums. Approved artists receive art coin airdrops to support their work. Approved art galleries receive tokens and free limited edition artworks from the founder. They can display and sell these artworks and accept $ART as a form of payment.
    Can $ART be used in museums?
    While you can't buy art from museums, $ART can be used for museum entrance fees and purchases at museum gift shops. The same offer extended to art galleries is sent to museums worldwide
    What makes this project unique?
    This project is unique as it combines cryptocurrency and art, benefiting artists, art galleries, museums, art investors, and the general public who love art. It aims to bridge the gap between the art world and cryptocurrency in a mutually beneficial way.
